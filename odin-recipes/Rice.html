Boiled rice

This is the rice advised by the doctors.

Doctors advised people not take fried rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 4kgs Rice
  • 1/4 spoon of salt

Steps

  1. Put the ready sorted rice in a saucepan
  2. Add the cups of water
  3. Add the salt
  4. Boil it for 10 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
