Potato Kugel

The best dish you can opt for.

As we all know best on our preferences. This is the best.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 10 medium potatoes, peeled and grated
  • 2 medium onions, peeled and grated
  • 5 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Step

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with 1 tablespoon vegetable oil.
  4. Combine grated potatoes and onions in a large bowl.
  5. Mix in eggs, 1/3 cup of vegetable oil, salt, and pepper until well combined; pour mixture into the prepared dish.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and crisp on top, 1 ½ to 2 hours.
